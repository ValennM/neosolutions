Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

When you hire our services, an email with your login information will be immediately sent to you. You will then enter that username and password into our Neo X platform, which is used to access all of your active services. It's never been easier!

Are refunds available?

If you believe a service doesn't meet your demands, you can ask for a refund in the 'Payments' section of our Neo X platform. Remember that refunds are subject to the terms of the payment platform.

Can I try a service first?

Absolutely! In our Plans section, you will find a 7-day trial with limited access to our Code Delivery and Code Counseling services, which will enable you to acquire first hand experience on how we work and the quality of our services.
